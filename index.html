    Vision

    Developed by Joshua Clarence Sadsad

    Hey there! Vision is a Data Visualization website that displays film information from the Marvel Cinematic Universe. In addition to Vanilla JavaScript, I used tools such as D3.js and the Intersection Observer API to deliver a smoother and more interactive experience. Please use the icons on the right for better navigation!
